02 / ZALNAE / 2025

Zalnae is a premium, oncology-safe, vegan and deeply nourishing haircare brand that combines the wisdom of tradition with a luxurious sensory experience. Their formulas are enriched with herbal decoctions, clays, natural oils, and perfumed microcapsules to deliver shine, volume, and scalp harmony — all without synthetic chemicals. The identity portrays a gentle and inviting atmosphere, making the brand feel like a tranquil ritual for people who want to engage more deeply in a stress-free part of their day.
